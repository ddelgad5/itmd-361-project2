Chicago Music

Music is such a large part of my life. Growing up, I had a love for alternative rock music and tried to listen to Q101 as much as possible. Alas, Q101 got shut down in 2011 and I didn't know of any other alternative (no pun intendid) radio stations. Eventually, I got taken into the internet streaming world and listened to music via Pandora. Originally it was mainly alternative rock, but then I got into a dubstep phase. From there, I moved onto hip-hop around 2014. Since then, my fondness of hip-hop has grown. I've learned to understand how the beat and poetry tranfuse and make this wonderful, head-bobbing, feeling. I actually started a radio show called "David's Vault" here at IIT. Although I can play any song I want, a majority of the content is hip-hop. Want to know about my favorite Chicago music artists?
